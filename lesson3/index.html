Site Purpose

The purpose of Happy Camper Weather Channel is to provide updated weather forecasts for any nature loving person whether they plan to go into the woods for a hike, or do some camping. Happycamperweather.com provides satellite updates that can be pushed to an electronic device of the user's choice. Website visitors can receive alerts and warnings for storms or other options tailored to their concerns so that they don't need to worry about a storm ruining their fun-filled day of activities.
