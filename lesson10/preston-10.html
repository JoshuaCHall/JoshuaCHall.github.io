Man saves rare Japanese fish that fell off a moving truck.


The small town of Preston, Idaho had a small dose of excitement this week when someone transporting a rare Japanese breed of koi fish almost lost their precious cargo. The fish was being transported in a secure tank strapped to the bed of their truck, however the straps had unknowingly loosened and the tank was dislodged when they traveled over a large bump in the road. A local man sitting at a local shop for his afternoon soda break noticed the tank tumble from the back of the truck and quickly leapt into action. Our local hero didn't know that the travelers had already notified police of the fish's disappearance, so he was almost arrested on theft charges upon the discovery of his possession of the koi. Everyone quickly uncovered the truth and all is well, with our local man now dubbed as the Preston koi hero.
