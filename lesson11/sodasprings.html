City moves forward with plans for water bottle factory


Big business could be coming to Soda Springs. The hightly acclaimed Earth-Springs water company is in talks with city officals about building a collection and processing plant nearby Bear Creek Springs. If the city is chosen for the new plant, it could bring 400 new jobs to the city of Soda Springs. Locals are very skeptical about the deal. They have their doubts about how environmentally sound the plan is. the Bear Creek Springs are under government protection from this exact thing. The company says that they received the go ahead from the government and their plan is legally sound. The towns folk are sill waiting to hear back from the city to see if everything is officially moving ahead.
